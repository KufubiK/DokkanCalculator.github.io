How to Use Both Calculators:

  1. Basics: Punch in the respective % from kits into the input field, and click the calculate button. Majority of these are quite self-explanatory, e.g.:
    • Double 220% lead → Put 440 in Leader %
    • Support memory that gives 10% → Put 10 in Support Memory %
  2. What's the difference between Start of Turn and Non Start of Turn?

    Start of Turn

    • ATK & DEF +X%
    • ATK & DEF +X% per ki/rainbow ki spheres obtained
    • ATK & DEF +X% per Y category ally on the team
    • ATK & DEF +X% when HP is Y% or above/below
    • ATK & DEF boost by up to +X% (the more/less HP remaining, the greater the ATK/DEF)
    • ATK & DEF +X% for Y turns from the start of battle
    • ATK & DEF +X% per existing enemy
    • ATK & DEF +X% when facing Y or more enemies
    • ATK & DEF +X% when Y ally is attacking in the same turn
    • ATK & DEF +X% when facing a Y category enemy/when facing Y enemy
    • ATK & DEF +X% when the character is the Yth to obtain Ki Spheres in a turn
    • ATK & DEF +X% with Y or more ki spheres obtained

    Non-Start of Turn

    • ATK & DEF +X% when attacking/performing a Super Attack/Ultra Super Attack
    • ATK & DEF +X% as Y attacker in a turn
    • ATK & DEF +X% when ki is Y or more
    • ATK & DEF +X% each time ki rises by one
    • ATK & DEF +X% when enemy is in the Atk down/Def down/Stunned/Sealed status
    • ATK & DEF +X% within the same turn/for X turns after receiving an attack
    • ATK & DEF +X% with each attack performed (only activates after the attack happens)
    • ATK & DEF +X% with each final blow delivered
  3. Ki and Super Attack Multiplier: These information are not directly shown in-game and must be found via external sources. For Ki Multiplier, you can use dokkaninfo.com and go to "Extra Info" on the character. For Super Attack Multiplier, you may use the look-up table below. Note, for characters that have stacking in their Super Attack Effect, you want to modify the Super Attack Multiplier (e.g. Raises ATK and causes immense damage, at SA Lv 10 it would be 505% (from the table) - 30% (To "compensate" for the Raise ATK) = 475%). Same applies to character who provide support on their super attack effect (e.g. Raises ATK & DEF for 1 turn and causes mega-colossal damage to enemy; raises Super Class allies' ATK by 50% for 1 turn, at SA 20 it would be 570% (from the table) - 50% (to "compensate" for the support) = 520%). The actual stacking and support from super then go to Super Attack Stacking and Super Attack Effect respectively.

  4. Super Attack Boost: Simply check your hidden potential and look for the icon that looks like a Ki Blast. There are typical values such as 55% having 6 Super Attack Boost and 100% having 15 Super Attack Boost (Not including any extra skill orbs that may provide more).
  • Note: All calculations are not 100% accurate due to the nature of how Dokkan calculates, and values may be off by the last digit.
